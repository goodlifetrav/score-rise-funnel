FAQ

Common Questions

What is the Score Rise Kit?

It's a digital education product: a Metro 2 audit worksheet, three dispute letter script templates, secondary bureau scripts, and a video masterclass — delivered instantly after purchase. One-time purchase with lifetime access. Not a subscription.

Do you file disputes on my behalf?

No. The kit teaches you how to do it yourself using your rights under the Fair Credit Reporting Act. You mail the letters, you track responses, you own the process.

Do you guarantee a specific score increase?

No. By federal law, no one can guarantee specific credit results. Results depend entirely on what's on your report and how the bureaus and furnishers respond to your disputes.

How will you contact me?

If you purchase, we'll send you transactional emails about your order, product access, and account. If you opt in to SMS through our forms, we'll also text you about order updates, customer support, and occasional product news. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for support.

Will my phone number be shared?

No. Mobile information (including phone numbers and SMS opt-in data) is never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
